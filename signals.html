Signals

Signals are external influences — people and ideas that have shaped how I think. Some are linked and sourced. Others are attributed from memory, where the source shaped me but may not have a URL. Both types are credited honestly.

    My greatest professional influence. The greatest higher education strategic marketing brain in Australia.

  • Influence Signal · NextEd, 1998

    Paul McKey gave me my first real break — and a belief that you could think your way out of any problem.

    A prodigious talent. Working alongside him raised my standard for what rigorous thinking actually looks like.
