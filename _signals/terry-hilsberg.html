Influence Signal · NextEd, 1998–2002

Terry Hilsberg

Terry Hilsberg was the CEO of NextEd. On a vacant floor of our new company, I built him an office out of the boxes from our first batch of servers. It had a door. He would crawl in and sit at his desk.

That detail tells you something about him — and about the era. We were building something genuinely new and the infrastructure was improvised. Terry was pragmatic, tough, and exceptionally high-expectation. You came to every conversation fully prepared or you didn't come. I never wanted to let him down.

What Terry modelled was what serious leadership looks like when the stakes are real and the path is uncharted. Forbes called NextEd one of a handful of front-runners in the new business of online education. Peter Drucker called online education the future. Terry was already building it.
