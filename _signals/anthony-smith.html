Influence Signal · USQ, CDU, UNE — 20+ years

Anthony Smith

Anthony Smith is my greatest professional influence. We worked together for over twenty years across three universities — USQ, Charles Darwin, and UNE — and he was my manager for much of that time.

Open, honest, collaborative, encouraging. The greatest higher education strategic marketing brain in Australia. He understood markets, brands, and student behaviour at a depth I have rarely seen matched anywhere. He commissioned decades of primary research into mature-age and online student markets when almost nobody else was paying attention to those cohorts.

What Anthony gave me was a standard. Not a set of rules — a standard for what excellent strategic thinking looks like, what genuine collaboration feels like, and what it means to lead people in a way that makes them better.

He also wrote one of my LinkedIn recommendations — the one that says I implement CRM and brand strategy at a level uncommon in most sectors. He would know.
