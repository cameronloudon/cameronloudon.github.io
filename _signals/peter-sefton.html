Influence Signal · NextEd & USQ, 2000–2007

Peter Sefton

I worked directly with Peter Sefton across two periods — at NextEd from 2000 to 2004, and again on the RUBRIC project at the University of Southern Queensland from 2006 to 2007.

Peter is a prodigious talent. Technically deep, intellectually restless, and one of those rare people who can hold a complex systems problem and a creative solution in his head simultaneously. Working alongside him raised my standard for what rigorous thinking actually looks like.
