Influence Signal · NextEd, 1998

Paul McKey

Paul McKey gave me my first real break. He was CTO and one of the founding employees of NextEd — one of the earliest global online education businesses — and I was one of the first handful of people through the door.

What Paul gave me wasn't a methodology or a framework. It was a belief: that you could think your way out of any problem. He was a genuine creative and innovator who approached obstacles as puzzles rather than blockers. That disposition lodged itself in me permanently. I still operate that way.
